C++ is my favourite programming language. I'm fascinated how it can be used to do very efficient and hardware related things and at the same time it is very good in abstracting things away. Starting in my last year of my bachelors studies, I program C++ for 4 years now. Besides my studies and my masters thesis, I took two online courses that are related to C++ during my educational career. One of them was the C++ for C programmers course at coursera and Udacity's C++ Nanodegree. Especially the last one was very helpful for my current job, since I learned a lot about memory management and multi threading.
